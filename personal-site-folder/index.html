Genshin Impact

INTRODUCTION

Genshin Impact is an action RPG (Action Role-Playing Game). At the beginning of the game, you can choose between a boy and a girl to be the main character (they are twins), the twin you didn't select is captured by an uncertain kind of goddess, after that, you wake up into an unknown world named Teyvat in which the people can control elements if they have a "Vision" that is a gift from one of the seven archons that rule this world. You will be able to control the elements without a vision and that is super uncommon. All of your adventures go around in looking for your lost sibling, you will meet a lot of people that will help you recollect hints of the parade of your sibling and at the same time, you will help them in their problems.

BASIC INFORMATION

Into the game, you will control elements, those elements are Pyro (Fire), Geo (Earth), Dendro (Nature), Cryo (Ice), Electro (Lightning), Anemo (Wind), and Hydro (Water). Another thing we need to keep in our minds is that with the elements that the characters control we can do elemental reactions. We need to understand that your characters and the enemies in the game can have these elements as a handicap, if one of your characters is affected by Hydro (Water) and then an enemy hits you with fire it will trigger the reaction "vaporize" and it will deal you more damage than only be hit by fire, the same applies for the enemies and we can use all of the different elements to do these reactions. The main character at first will be an Anemo (Wind) user. All the characters will use a type of weapon, the weapons are Sword, Bow, Polearm (Lance), Claymore (Two-handed sword), and Catalyst (Books of mage), characters can only use one.
