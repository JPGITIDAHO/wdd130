Welcome

The people who will visit our page will access information and motivation to start a design project or acquire notions to improve or start their micro-enterprise. We all at some point have the desire to create something but we don't know how to do it. One of the reasons why this page may be more visited than others is that my page is a page of free services that are usually found on the web but are paid. The basic information that will be required is an email to later be able to follow up and encourage them to continue with the progress within the page. In addition to giving them a welcome, it will motivate them to be self-sufficient. Another reason for this course is that currently, the work of the carpenter is recognized as essential for the construction industry. His experience and talent allow him to cover a number of different areas, including proper foundation positioning, new stair placement, joist installation, trim, decorations, and more.

The questions that my page will basically answer are what is it like to be a carpenter, what and what are the types of wood, and how to start and design your projects in two types of wood. I will show in a simple way what are the necessary tools and what basic skills those who want to start should have. We will also include tips and basic information for managing microenterprise resources that are not freely available on most web pages. This section will answer essential questions about the proper functioning of a microenterprise, be it financial, as well as the good treatment of employees if they have any. Some of the questions we will answer will be what are the first steps and tips to improve your small business
