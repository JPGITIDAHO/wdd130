Content

To giving a welcome

People will visit our page because the message is clear and for specific people who want to learn or start their small business. We all at some point have the desire to create something but we don't know how to do it. One of the reasons why this page may be more visited than others is that my page is a page of free services that are generally found on the web but in a paid way. The basic information that will be required is an email to later be able to follow up and encourage them to continue with the progress within the page. In addition to giving a welcome, it will motivate them to be self-sufficient.

Images for the Home page

How you can learn how to design furniture

The questions that my page will basically answer are how you can learn how to design furniture, what are the necessary tools and what basic skills those who want to start should have. Links will be released for it. Basic step-by-step designs will also be shown for people to create at home. through images. Software that people can use to optimize cuts in wooden plates will be released. Tips on how to optimize interior spaces with furniture made by themselves will also be released.

Images for the Page 2

The proper functioning of a microenterprise

Management of microenterprise resources that are not freely accessible on most web pages. In this section, essential questions will be answered regarding the proper functioning of a microenterprise, be it financial, as well as the good treatment of employees, if they had any. What are the first steps and what are the next steps to follow with your own business. Training will be carried out and then some brief questionnaires to strengthen learning.
