To giving a welcome

The people who will visit our page will access information and motivation to start a design project or acquire notions to improve or start their micro-enterprise. We all at some point have the desire to create something but we don't know how to do it. One of the reasons why this page may be more visited than others is that my page is a page of free services that are usually found on the web but are paid. The basic information that will be required is an email to later be able to follow up and encourage them to continue with the progress within the page. In addition to giving them a welcome, it will motivate them to be self-sufficient. Another reason for this course is that currently, the work of the carpenter is recognized as essential for the construction industry. His experience and talent allow him to cover a number of different areas, including proper foundation positioning, new stair placement, joist installation, trim, decorations, and more. The questions that my page will basically answer are what is it like to be a carpenter, what and what are the types of wood, and how to start and design your projects in two types of wood. I will show in a simple way what are the necessary tools and what basic skills those who want to start should have. We will also include tips and basic information for managing microenterprise resources that are not freely available on most web pages. This section will answer essential questions about the proper functioning of a microenterprise, be it financial, as well as the good treatment of employees if they have any. Some of the questions we will answer will be what are the first steps and tips to improve your small business

Learning and Design

Learning will be from scratch. I will talk about the nature of carpentry. I will include basic carpentry concepts. The different types of wood and how to differentiate them. I'll start with questions and answers about what tools and materials I should have. How to create my workspace. How to organize my workshop. Since there are several types of wood that can be learned to use, in this section we will show how to use two of them. Hardwood will be the material we will use for our first project. The project that I will include will be how to make a meat chopping board. The first part will indicate what types of hardwood can be obtained and what are the basic dimensions that must be met for the project. This is for starters, you can always include your own imagination as long as you stick to the basic rules. They will then need to gather the necessary materials and tools for this project. It will tell you what they are since although it is a simple project, specific materials are required to do it correctly. I will include a list. The second part of the project will be the preparation that will be divided into 8 steps with images and texts. The third part will include the realization of the project in 8 steps, we will see how we have to finish. The second option is in melamine chipboard. This type of material is much easier to start and finish as it requires fewer processes in its treatment. This means that since the material comes with a ready-made finishing process, only cutting and finishing steps are required in the assembly, unlike rustic wood. This project will be like making a nightstand and include a drawer. The project will be shown step by step in this type of material. The first part will be to design and choose the color. I will include a link to design in a specialized software the dimensioning of this material, the program is called Polyboard. The second part will include the breakdown of this project. The third part will include the preparation of the material before assembly. Then we will go to the assembly and finally to the completion including optional details.

Small-business solutions

This section will include hints and tips to help small businesses thrive. We may be the most competent accountants, resume writers, or artisan bakers in the world, but your small business will eventually suffer if it lacks business sense. Starting your own small business is an exercise in uncertainty, which is why it's so important to learn from those who have been in your shoes. With the right guidance, you will be successful in creating a unique business. You'll run into roadblocks that can threaten business viability if you overlook critical administrative tasks like bookkeeping or maintaining high-quality supplier relationships. In fact, the top reasons a new business fails include lacking a marketing strategy, not having a plan for how to scale the business to meet growing demand, or offering a poorly conceived product or service with a total addressable market (TAM). ) too small. Here we will learn financial and marketing tips, as well as tips to maintain a good relationship with your employees.
